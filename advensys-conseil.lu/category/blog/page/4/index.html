Les avantages de la comptabilité informatisée

En plus de la bonne humeur qui y règne ,

Il sera rapide et simple de s’intégrer dans l’équipe de Groupe Advensys Luxembourg S.A surtout avec le logiciel Ciel Compta Pro qui est un logiciel performant et simple à utiliser.
Quand je dis simple, il vous suffit d’une semaine pour pouvoir manipuler le logiciel a 100%, et si vous ne pensez pas être à la hauteur ou bien vous ne pensez pas possible alors je vous laisse relever le défi !
Nous allons vous décrire un peu le logiciel pour avoir un avant-gout.
Pour commencer, il existe plusieurs modes de saisie sur le logiciel qui vont vous permettre avec faciliter l’entrée des factures d’achat, de ventes ou des extraits bancaires et cela sans complication et avec rapidité.
Vous pouvez travailler sur plusieurs exercices sans clôture définitive du dossier même en cours,
le logiciel calcul également automatiquement la TVA, le solde des écritures et du compte de contrepartie.
Pour finir, le logiciel vous assiste lors de la saisie des relevés bancaire avec le Lettrage/pointage automatique des factures pour un suivi financier simple et rapide.

Miodrag Milic,
Responsable comptabilité sociétés
Advensys Conseil

Luxembourg Tax return for individuals : list of documents

Here we are going to show you what  details and necessary documents

you have to be aware before completing successful tax return in Luxembourg.

 

First of all  your PERSONAL INFORMATION.

Last name

First Name

Date of birth

Luxembourg Social Security Number

Profession

Phone

Email

Current address

previous address in case of relocation since 01.01.2015

Name of bank account owner

IBAN

SWIFT BIC

Date of marriage
Date of the Civil partnership
Date of divorce/separation

Children info

Please precise if you receive alimony for a child who is part of your household or if you receive alimony for a child who is not part of your household.

 

LIST OF DOCUMENTS (Copies)

1. All your annual income certificates from Luxembourg and abroad

 

2. Certificates of interest expenses related to your mortgage loan for your primary residence

 

3. Income certificates of property income (dividends, ..) fr. Luxembourg and also abroad

 

4. Proof of payment of alimony to former spouse or children / copy of judgment (if applicable)

 

5. Interest expenses certificates of personal loans (or credit cards) granted within the EU

 

6. Certificates of civil liability and life insurance premiums (EU)

life (health, accident, disability, sickness …)
civil liability (cl car, cl housing, any other cl)
certificates should only contain civil liability premiums

 

7. Proof of contributions to private health insurance

 

8. Certificates related to private old-age pension scheme (111bis LIR)

 

9. Statements of EU Housing Savings Plan

 

9. Statements of EU Housing Savings Plan

 

11. Certificates of donations to charity organisations

 

12. Certificates of child care expenses

 

13. Certificates of household employee expenses

 

14. Certificates supporting extraordinary expenses ( healthcare, financial assistance to parents in need, funeral,…)

 

15. Certificates of interest expenses related to mortgage loans related to a property for rent

 

15b. Receipts for rent received, a copy of the deed of purchase and notary invoice

 

15b. Receipts for rent received, a copy of the deed of purchase and notary invoice

 

16. Certificates of RSU / SHARE granted as part of compensation

 

17. If person of Foreign origin, the related filed Income tax return and income details

 

18. Any Other investment income details coming from Shares or Mutual Funds or Bonds etc.

 

19. Your prior year Luxembourg Tax filing details (Return and Assessment).

tax advice and tax simulation.

Coaching

About moving towards achieving your goals and the success you desire

Advensys Conseil Coaching Programs  use modern applications and links  to deliver the best results for the clients.

 

We are easily distracted and often fall into the trap of living by different priorities every day. Advensys Conseil Coaching Programs provide you with the structure to create a concrete plan, stay focused on your goals and most important tasks, monitor your progress, resolve problems, and overcome obstacles. By sticking to a clear and consistent course of action you become much more productive and effective. You just need to show up and act on your plan every day and success will be yours!

 

Advensys Conseil Coaching Programs can be divided into three sections that help you stay focused, be more productive and accomplish more:

 

The Morning Routine will prompt you to look at the day ahead, and make sure that your actions are aligned with your goals. You’ll brainstorm ideas, set targets and visualize your future success.

 

Throughout the day, the All-Day Action Plan reminds you of your planned activities and commitments. It also allows you to take notes and record your insights as you work, so you can review and process them later.

 

The End-of-Day Routine allows you to reflect on your day, learn from it, and plan your following day.

 

By going through the guided Advensys Conseil Coaching Programs process every day, you will consistently be moving towards achieving your goals and the success you desire!
